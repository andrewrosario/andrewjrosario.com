A long, long time ago (in the '90s) before online games were much of a think. All we had were console platformers and side-scrollers. With my relatively poor hand-eye corrdination and reaction speed, this was not a good time in gaming for me. That was until my friends and I discovered MUDs. Mult-user dungeons, were an extension of the Dungeons and Dragons we were already playing and a pre-cursor of todays MMORPGs. I fell in love and my first experiences with coding were tinkering with open source MUD code and trying to build my own.
